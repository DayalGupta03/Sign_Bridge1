# SignBridge 3D: Business Model & Market Viability

## Executive Summary
SignBridge 3D is a B2B SaaS platform providing real-time, AI-mediated communication infrastructure for healthcare providers. By replacing expensive on-call interpreters with instant, always-available AI mediation, we reduce hospital wait times, improve patient outcomes, and ensure ADA/ACA compliance.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Enterprise Licensing (Hospitals & Chains)
*   **Model**: Annual recurring license per facility + usage tiers.
*   **Pricing**: $25k/year base + $0.50 per mediation minute.
*   **Value Prop**: 70% cost reduction compared to human interpreters ($150-$300/hour minimums).

### 2. Hardware-as-a-Service (HaaS)
*   **Model**: Lease dedicated "Bridge Terminals" (iPads/Kiosks with optimized mics/cameras).
*   **Pricing**: $200/month per terminal.
*   **Context**: Deploying into ERs and triaging stations for instant accessibility.

### 3. API Access (Telehealth Integration)
*   **Model**: Usage-based API for integrating SignBridge into existing telehealth platforms (Teladoc, Amwell).
*   **Pricing**: volume-based metering (e.g., $0.05 per API call).

## Market Analysis

### Problem Size
*   **70 Million+** Deaf people globally (WFD).
*   **Misdiagnosis Risk**: Deaf patients are **3x more likely** to be misdiagnosed in ERs due to communication barriers.
*   **Compliance Costs**: US Hospitals spend over **$4B annually** on language services to meet federal mandates (ACA Section 1557).

### Target Audience (B2B)
1.  **Emergency Departments**: High urgency, need zero-latency communication.
2.  **Urgent Care Clinics**: High volume, cost-sensitive.
3.  **Specialized Telehealth Platforms**: Need accessibility features to expand market share.

## Go-to-Market Strategy

### Phase 1: Pilot Programs (Months 1-6)
*   Partner with 3 teaching hospitals for beta testing.
*   Focus on "Triage" use case (intake forms, basic symptom functioning).
*   Metric: "Time to Understanding" reduction.

### Phase 2: Regional Expansion (Months 7-18)
*   Integrate with Epic/Cerner EHR systems to save transcripts directly to patient records.
*   Sales focus on hospital CIOs and Diversity/Inclusion officers.

### Phase 3: Telehealth Integration (Months 18+)
*   SDK release for mobile apps.
*   Licensing technology to major telemedicine providers.
